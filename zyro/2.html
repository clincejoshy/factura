ABOUT

Factura is an annual National level Conference of NSS College of Engineering Palakkad, started in 2015 (FACTURA 2K15) with the aim of providing a platform for scholars and professionals to develop and showcase their technical prowess. The activities were conducted as technical events in a two day programme at NSSCE campus. The first edition of FACTURA had the honour to be sponsored by MALABAR CEMENTS LIMITED & INSTRUMENTATION LIMITED.
NSS College of engineering, Palakkad established in 1960, is one of the leading institutions in the field of technical education in Kerala. The government aided, Nationally Accredited Institution is situated in the enchanting foothills of Western Ghats and is accessible in the easiest way with 9 km from Palakkad town and 4 km from the Palakkad Jn. Railway station, The institute has well equipped laboratories and highly qualified faculty members with a broad spectrum of specializations. The institute strives to groom professionals of high standard catering to global need.
The Department of Mechanical Engineering has a support of highly qualified and specialized faculty. Since its inception in 1960, it has become a synonym for brilliance. The department is accredited by AICTE and is one of the most sought after branches in the state. The department offers an emerging thrust area M.Tech Programme with specialization in Computer Integrated manufacturing. The focus of this topic is on all functions in Design, Manufacturing, Quality control and Process Control & Management which ultimately lead to complete automation of a manufacturing enterprise.
